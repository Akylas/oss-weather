# Daily Forecasts

Extended weather forecasts for the week ahead and beyond.

## Overview

Daily forecasts provide a summary of expected weather conditions for each day, helping you plan activities days or weeks in advance.

## Forecast Range

Depending on your weather provider:
- **OpenWeather**: 8 days
- **Open-Meteo**: 16 days
- **AccuWeather**: 15 days (on free tier, limited detail)
- **Meteo France**: 14 days

## Daily Weather Data

For each day, you'll see:

### Temperature
- **High Temperature** - Maximum expected temperature
- **Low Temperature** - Minimum expected temperature
- **Average Temperature** - Mean temperature (some providers)

### Conditions
- **Weather Description** - Overall conditions (sunny, cloudy, rainy, etc.)
- **Weather Icon** - Visual representation
- **Condition Details** - More specific description

### Precipitation
- **Probability** - Chance of precipitation (%)
- **Expected Amount** - Rainfall/snowfall amount
- **Type** - Rain, snow, or mixed

### Wind
- **Average Wind Speed** - Typical wind speed
- **Maximum Gusts** - Strongest expected gusts
- **Wind Direction** - Predominant direction

### Other Metrics
- **Humidity** - Average humidity level
- **UV Index** - Maximum UV exposure
- **Cloud Cover** - Expected cloudiness
- **Sunrise/Sunset** - Sun times for the day

## Reading Daily Forecasts

### Temperature Range

**Wide Range** (e.g., 10°C to 25°C):
- Expect significant temperature variation
- Morning/evening cool, midday warm
- Plan for layered clothing

**Narrow Range** (e.g., 20°C to 23°C):
- Stable temperature throughout day
- Overcast or cloudy conditions likely
- Consistent conditions expected

### Precipitation Probability

- **0-20%**: Unlikely to rain
- **20-40%**: Slight chance, usually okay to plan outdoor activities
- **40-60%**: Moderate chance, have backup plans
- **60-80%**: Likely to rain, indoor activities recommended
- **80-100%**: Rain very likely, plan accordingly

### Weather Patterns

Look for patterns across multiple days:
- **Improving trend**: Temperatures rising, rain decreasing
- **Worsening trend**: Temperatures falling, rain increasing
- **Stable pattern**: Consistent conditions
- **Variable**: Changing day-to-day

## Tips & Tricks

### Best Days for Activities

Look for:
- 0-20% precipitation
- Moderate temperatures
- Low wind speeds
- Good UV index for outdoor activities

### Worst Days

Avoid:
- >60% precipitation probability
- Extreme temperatures
- High wind speeds
- Severe weather alerts

### Temperature Planning

**Cold Days** (Below comfortable range):
- Check feels-like temperature
- Consider wind chill
- Plan indoor alternatives

**Hot Days** (Above comfortable range):
- Check UV index
- Plan for heat
- Stay hydrated
- Avoid midday sun

### Precipitation Planning

**Rain Expected**:
- Indoor activities
- Rain gear ready
- Drainage check
- Delay outdoor projects

**Snow Expected**:
- Travel preparations
- Heating check
- Winter gear
- Stock supplies

## Limitations

### Long-Range Accuracy

Forecasts beyond 7 days:
- Show general trends only
- Less reliable for specific planning
- Subject to significant changes
- Use for general awareness only

### Local Variations

Forecasts are for broad areas:
- Microclimates may differ
- Mountains/valleys have variations
- Urban vs rural differences
- Coastal effects

### Changing Forecasts

Forecasts update regularly:
- Check daily for important events
- Closer dates get more accurate
- Sudden changes can occur
- Always recheck 1-2 days before

## Daily vs Hourly

**Daily Forecasts**:
- Good for: Multi-day planning, general overview
- Shows: Day-level summaries
- Range: Up to 16 days

**Hourly Forecasts**:
- Good for: Same-day planning, precise timing
- Shows: Hour-by-hour details
- Range: Up to 48 hours

Use both: Daily for planning, hourly for execution.
