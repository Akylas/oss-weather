# Weather Comparison

Compare weather conditions between multiple locations side-by-side.

## Overview

The weather comparison feature allows you to view and compare weather data for multiple locations simultaneously, making it easy to decide between destinations or track weather across different areas.

## Accessing Weather Comparison

1. Tap the **Compare** tab in the bottom navigation
2. Select locations to compare
3. View side-by-side comparison

## Comparison Views

### Current Conditions

Compare real-time weather:
- Temperature
- Feels-like temperature
- Weather conditions
- Precipitation
- Wind speed
- Humidity
- Pressure

### Temperature Comparison

Visual comparison of:
- Current temperatures
- High/low for the day
- Temperature trends
- Color-coded differences

### Precipitation Comparison

Compare:
- Chance of rain
- Precipitation amount
- Precipitation type
- Radar differences

### Forecast Comparison

Compare future forecasts:
- Hourly forecasts
- Daily forecasts
- Temperature trends
- Weather patterns

## Tips & Tricks

### Effective Comparison

**Choose Relevant Metrics**:
- Focus on what matters for your decision
- Too much data can be overwhelming
- Prioritize key factors

**Consider Timeframe**:
- Current for immediate decisions
- Hourly for today's planning
- Daily for week planning
- Extended for travel

**Check Multiple Providers**:
- Different forecasts possible
- Compare for accuracy
- Consider local providers

### Decision Making

**Temperature**:
- Consider your comfort range
- Factor in humidity (feels-like)
- Check for extremes

**Precipitation**:
- Look at probability AND amount
- Consider activity impact
- Check timing (hourly)

**Overall Conditions**:
- Balance multiple factors
- Consider priorities
- Check alternatives

- May need to scroll
